Content Area

This is where your actual content (text, images, etc.) lives. It's the innermost part of the box.

Padding

Padding is the space *between* the content and the border. It pushes the border outwards from the content.

Border

The border is a line that goes around the padding and content. You can style its width, style (solid, dashed), and color.

Margin

Margin is the space *outside* the border. It creates space between the element's border and other elements around it.
